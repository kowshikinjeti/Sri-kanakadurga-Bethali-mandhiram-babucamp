About us

Sri Kanaka Durgamma Mandiram

The sacred Sri Kanaka Durgamma Temple stands as a divine symbol of devotion and faith for over 50+ years. Established by devoted villagers, the temple has been a guiding spiritual force for thousands of devotees who seek the blessings of Durga Mata.

Every year, the temple celebrates the grand Durga Mata Navratri and Dasara Utsavam with immense devotion and cultural unity. In 2025, the Dasara Festival was successfully celebrated with traditional poojas, alankarams, bhajans, and colorful decorations that filled the air with spirituality and joy.
